Step 1 of 4
🗄️
Set up your Notion page
Create or pick a page in Notion where your bookmarks will be saved. We'll create the database table for you automatically.
  • 1 Open Notion and create a new page (or use an existing one).
  • 2 Name it something like "Saved Bookmarks".
  • 3 That's it! You'll paste the page link in Step 3 and we'll set up the table automatically.
✨ The extension will auto-create a database inside your page with the correct Title, URL, and Source columns. No manual setup needed!
Already have a database?
  • If you already created a Notion database with Title, URL, and Source columns, just paste its link in Step 3 — we'll detect it automatically.
Step 2 of 4
🔗
Create and connect a Notion connection
A connection lets this extension access your database.
  • Part A: Create the connection
  • 1 Go to: Notion connections ↗
  • 2 Click + New Connection.
  • 3 Name it "Bookmark Saver".
  • 4 Click Submit.
  • 5 Copy the Access token (inside Integration Token section, starts with ntn_ or secret_).
  • Part B: Add it to your database
  • 1 Open your database from Step 1.
  • 2 Click the ⋯ (three dots) menu in the top right.
  • 3 Click Add connections.
  • 4 Search for "Bookmark Saver" and select it. Click Confirm.
Need help?
  • 📖 Read & Watch: Add and manage connections ↗
